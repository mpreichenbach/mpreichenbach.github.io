As of February 2021, I work as a Research Mathematician at the Geospatial Research Laboratory, in Alexandria, VA. I develop deep-learning models to do land-cover classification of high-resolution imagery, and create data analysis tools and movement models to better understand how invasive carp react to acoustic deterrents.

I did my PhD work at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, where I was advised by Richard Rebarber and Brigitte Tenhumberg.
